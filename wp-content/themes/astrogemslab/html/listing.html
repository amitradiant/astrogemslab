Blue Sapphire(Neelam)

Blue Sapphire (Neelam) is an extremely valuable, blue coloured gemstone of the Corundum mineral family. Known as the most influential and active acting gemstone in Vedic astrology, it is very effective in Business, Career, Health & Marriage and achievement in a person’s life. Natural blue sapphire (Neelam) generally comes from Sri-Lanka(Ceylon), Burma & Kashmir and is worn for planet Saturn (Shani). The stone is extremely helpful for individuals experiencing a tough period of ‘Shani Sadhe Saati’ or ‘Dhaiya’ in their lives.
